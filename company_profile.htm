Company Profile

ACPL is the multi-disciplinary consulting organization, offering a range of integrated and innovative and high quality services to international donors, governments and corporate across various development sectors and practices.
Since its inception in 1986, ACPL has to its credit undertaking several challenging assignments. ACPL partners with governments and development agencies to provide innovative solutions and support towards more inclusive, equitable and sustainable world
ACPL started its operation in 1981 in Libya. It handled assignments related to project management and consultancy for turn-key projects. In 1986, Anuj International was merged with ACPL GLOBAL P. Ltd., a private limited Company, incorporated in 1986 under the Indian Companies Act, 1956. The company since then has worked in all fields of Roads and Highways, Civil and Structural Engineering works including preparation of feasibility reports, DPRs, design, construction and supervision etc. It stands out as providing opportunity for Technological and Engineering excellence in Engineering fields.
Over the years, ACPL has progressively widened its expert capabilities from Roads and Highways, Bridges, Water Resources Development, Sewerage and Waste Water, Municipal Engineering and Transport Engineering to Irrigation, Environmental Planning & Management, Rural, Urban and Social Development, Training and Capacity Building, and Monitoring & Supervision of Construction Projects.
ACPL’s services have expanded and extend through all stages of the project life cycle including, pre-feasibility and feasibility studies, field investigations, laboratory testing, computer modelling, design, preparation of tender and contract documents, tender evaluation, contract management and construction supervision, quality assurance, commissioning, operation and maintenance, training and overall project management. ACPL provides quality professional services and delivers innovative solutions.
The Company executed various projects in Roads, Bridges and Highways, water supply, water treatment plants, sewerage, sewerage pumping stations, schools, industries, roads and culverts, turnkey projects of Government and Institutes, City Development Plans and other related components of infrastructure under JNNURM, UDSMT and IDSMT of Government of India.
ACPL has dedicated and experienced team of planners, designers, construction engineers and development specialists, who are ever ready to contribute their expertise together and turn vision into reality. It has achieved position of one of the leading consulting organization in India. ACPL’s in-house capabilities in technology development are complemented by tie-ups with various other organizations. It provides state-of-the-art products and solutions to a large and diverse customer base.
ACPL is proficient in rendering quality consulting services primarily in the field of Civil Engineering. ACPL specializes in Project Planning and Design; Project Management; Surveying; Urban Services; and Geographic Information Systems Development. ACPL is known for its excellence in Sewerage, Highways, Bridges, Railways, Water Resources and Irrigation, Urban Infrastructure and other allied sectors. ACPL aims to expand the scope and extent of present activities further, giving utmost importance to quality and accuracy of the projects that are undertaken. The past performance demonstrates that the company has achieved its objectives of quality and accuracy in every job.
